痛点 1 · 原生模态约束

Pain 1 · Native Modality Constraint

只有纯表格数据，无文本/图像/图谱。传统序列化方案缺乏底层连续性，需要原生数值敏感编码。

Pure tabular data only — no text/images/graphs. Naive textualization lacks numerical continuity; native tabular encoding is needed.

🔀

Pain 2 · Heterogeneous Projection Alignment

源域A与目标域B列名完全不同、d_A ≠ d_B、分布漂移极大，传统迁移学习完全失效。

Source A vs. target B: completely different column names, d_A ≠ d_B, extreme distribution shift — traditional TL completely breaks down.

❄️

目标域B仅50条样本，需激活LLM从A域习得的通用信贷先验，实现零样本/少样本降维打击。

Only 50 samples in domain B; must activate credit priors learned from domain A to achieve zero/few-shot inference.

TabPFN v2: Improved In-Context Learning for Tabular Data

Noah Hollmann, Samuel Müller, Lennart Purucker, et al. · NeurIPS 2024 (arXiv 2410.03013)
TabPFN v2将先验数据拟合网络（Prior-Data Fitted Network）扩展至更大规模，通过在数百万合成数据集上预训练ICL-Transformer，实现对任意新数据集的零样本预测。其核心突破在于将单一模型的推断过程等价于"贝叶斯最优预测器在合成先验下的后验推断"，从而无需目标域任何梯度更新即可直接分类。论文在300+真实Benchmark数据集上全面超越XGBoost与CatBoost，将ICL范式推向工业可用门槛。
TabPFN v2 scales Prior-Data Fitted Networks to larger scale, pretraining an ICL-Transformer on millions of synthetic datasets to achieve zero-shot prediction on any new dataset. Its key insight equates single-forward-pass inference to Bayesian-optimal prediction under a synthetic prior — no gradient updates needed on the target domain. Outperforms XGBoost/CatBoost on 300+ benchmarks, pushing ICL to industrial applicability.

UniTabE: A Universal Pretraining Protocol for Tabular Foundation Models in Data Science

Yazheng Yang, Yuqi Wang, Guang Liu, et al. · NeurIPS 2023 (arXiv 2307.09249)
UniTabE提出了一个突破固定Schema约束的通用表格预训练协议。其核心模块TabUnit将每个"列名-数值对"编码为语义向量，再经Transformer精炼，形成不依赖具体列结构的统一表示。该框架支持增量列学习，并将预训练知识跨任务迁移，为解决d_A ≠ d_B的跨域问题提供了关键方法论依据。
UniTabE proposes a universal tabular pretraining protocol breaking fixed-schema constraints. The TabUnit module encodes each (column-name, value) pair as a semantic vector refined by Transformer, forming structure-agnostic representations. Supports incremental column learning and cross-task transfer — a key methodological basis for d_A ≠ d_B cross-domain alignment.

Revisiting Deep Learning Models for Tabular Data (FT-Transformer)

Widely used baseline; its column-level tokenization design is a key starting point for heterogeneous alignment works (UniTabE, CARTE, etc.).

CARTE targets the "no-correspondence" challenge in cross-table transfer. Represents tables as graphs (rows as nodes, columns as edges), uses graph-attentional networks to contextualize column names and entries — enabling joint pretraining and transfer across tables with completely different columns, without schema matching. Directly addresses d_A ≠ d_B.

【中文版】

学术界对大模型处理纯表格数据的形式化界定已形成基本共识。Gorishniy 等（NeurIPS 2021）通过大规模系统实验指出，一个完备的深度表格模型应当能够克服三大归纳偏置缺陷：对无关特征的不鲁棒性、无法保持数据方向性以及难以学习不规则函数。在此基础上，FT-Transformer将每列特征映射为连续Token向量，建立了"列级Token化→Transformer上下文融合→分类/回归"的原生表格深度学习范式，成为后续所有表格基础模型的方法论起点。Yang 等（NeurIPS 2023，UniTabE）进一步将其抽象为"任意Schema无关预训练"框架：将每个基本表格元素（列名-数值对）封装为可迁移的TabUnit模块，经统一Transformer编码后形成与具体列结构解耦的潜在表示，从而支持增量列学习与跨数据集迁移，为解决 $d_A \neq d_B$ 的跨域异构问题奠定了结构性基础。

Hollmann 等（NeurIPS 2024，TabPFN v2）则将整个表格推断过程等价为贝叶斯最优预测器在合成先验下的后验推断：通过在数百万合成数据集上离线拟合先验分布，TabPFN v2在测试阶段以单次前向传播即可完成"元学习式"预测，无需任何梯度更新。McElfresh 等（NeurIPS 2023）通过对比19种算法跨176个数据集的最大规模表格Benchmark测试，以实证方式验证了TabPFN在小样本场景（≤3000条训练数据）下全面领先所有对手的事实，直接为信用评分冷启动场景中的算法选型提供了量化依据。

在异构特征对齐方向，Kim 等（ICML 2024，CARTE）针对跨表迁移中的"无结构对应"痛点，提出将关系型表格表示为图结构，利用图注意力网络同步编码列名语义与条目值，从而无需任何预设的Schema匹配即可实现异构表格间的联合预训练——这直接对标了源域A与目标域B"列名完全不同"的核心工程挑战。Wen 等（KDD 2024，GTL）则以生成式范式为切入点，将LLaMA-2在384个真实表格数据集上进行跨领域预训练，赋予其捕捉数值序列依赖性与跨域统计关系的能力，并在有限数据场景下展现出显著的少样本泛化优势，从实证层面论证了"通过生成式预训练构建跨域通用信贷先验"的可行性。

在大模型表格ICL方向，Gardner 等（NeurIPS 2023 Workshop，TABLET）系统性评估了GPT系列模型在纯表格预测任务上的ICL能力，发现通过精心设计的特征描述注入与少样本示例选择策略，LLM可在无文本特征的条件下实现有效的表格分类，同时将数值推理能力的不足界定为当前LLM表格处理的核心瓶颈。van Breugel 等（2024，TabuLa-8B）进一步将Llama 3-8B在包含21亿行、400万张异构表格的TabLib语料上进行微调，在329个测试集上实现了超越随机猜测15个百分点的零样本准确率，从大规模实验角度证明了语言模型在不依赖任何文本特征的条件下仍能从纯数值结构中习得通用表格先验。

在决策闭环与金融应用方向，Lewis 等（NeurIPS 2020）提出的RAG框架以"检索-读取-生成"三阶段流水线为表格样本检索增强提供了方法论原型；Wei 等（NeurIPS 2022）发现的CoT涌现能力表明LLM可通过中间推理步骤展示逻辑链条，为信贷决策的规制可解释性提供了内生机制。综合而言，以UniTabE、CARTE、GTL为代表的"异构对齐-联合预训练-跨域微调"序列化逻辑，与TabPFN、TabuLa-8B所代表的"大规模合成/真实先验拟合-零样本ICL推断"路径，共同构成了当前表格基础模型领域的两大主流技术范式，为本文所提出的两篇论文提供了坚实的方法论基础与清晰的创新窗口。

[English Version]

The academic community has reached a basic consensus on formalizing LLM-based processing of pure tabular data. Gorishniy et al. (NeurIPS 2021) identified through large-scale systematic experiments that a complete deep tabular model must overcome three fundamental inductive bias flaws: non-robustness to uninformative features, failure to preserve data orientation, and difficulty learning irregular functions. Building on this, FT-Transformer established the paradigm of "column-level tokenization → Transformer contextual fusion → prediction," creating the canonical native tabular deep learning baseline. Yang et al. (NeurIPS 2023, UniTabE) further abstracted this into a "schema-agnostic universal pretraining" framework: encapsulating each (column-name, value) pair into transferable TabUnit modules, forming column-structure-decoupled latent representations that support incremental column learning and cross-dataset transfer — providing a structural foundation for resolving d_A ≠ d_B cross-domain heterogeneity.

Hollmann et al. (NeurIPS 2024, TabPFN v2) equated the entire tabular inference process with Bayesian-optimal posterior inference under a synthetic prior: by offline fitting the prior distribution over millions of synthetic datasets, TabPFN v2 accomplishes "meta-learning style" prediction via a single forward pass at test time — no gradient updates required. McElfresh et al. (NeurIPS 2023) empirically validated, through the largest tabular benchmark (19 algorithms × 176 datasets), that TabPFN comprehensively outperforms all competitors in small-data settings (≤3000 training samples), directly quantifying the algorithm selection rationale for cold-start credit scoring.

In heterogeneous feature alignment, Kim et al. (ICML 2024, CARTE) tackled the "no structural correspondence" challenge in cross-table transfer by representing relational tables as graphs, using graph-attentional networks to simultaneously encode column-name semantics and entry values — enabling joint pretraining across heterogeneous tables without any predefined schema matching, directly targeting the "completely different column names" engineering challenge. Wen et al. (KDD 2024, GTL) pre-trained LLaMA-2 across 384 real tabular datasets in a generative paradigm, demonstrating remarkable few-shot generalization in limited-data scenarios — empirically validating the feasibility of building universal credit priors through generative cross-domain pretraining.

In the LLM tabular ICL direction, Gardner et al. (NeurIPS 2023 Workshop, TABLET) systematically evaluated GPT models' ICL capabilities on pure tabular prediction, finding that carefully designed feature description injection and few-shot example selection enable effective tabular classification without text features. van Breugel et al. (2024, TabuLa-8B) fine-tuned Llama 3-8B on 2.1B rows from 4M heterogeneous tables, achieving zero-shot accuracy 15pp above random on 329 test datasets. In the decision loop direction, the RAG framework of Lewis et al. (NeurIPS 2020) provides the methodological prototype for tabular sample retrieval augmentation, while the CoT emergence of Wei et al. (NeurIPS 2022) provides intrinsic explainability mechanisms for regulatory credit decision interpretation. Collectively, the "heterogeneous alignment → joint pretraining → cross-domain fine-tuning" sequential logic (UniTabE, CARTE, GTL) and the "large-scale prior fitting → zero-shot ICL inference" pathway (TabPFN v2, TabuLa-8B) constitute the two dominant paradigms in tabular foundation model research — providing solid methodological foundations and clear innovation opportunities for the two proposed papers.

🔬 HiTA: Heterogeneous-column-aware In-context Tabular Aligner for Cross-Domain Credit Scoring via Latent Semantic Projection

🔬 HiTA: Heterogeneous-column-aware In-context Tabular Aligner for Cross-Domain Credit Scoring via Latent Semantic Projection

目标投稿 · KDD 2025 / AAAI 2025 · 底层架构方向
Target Venue · KDD 2025 / AAAI 2025 · Core Architecture Track

🎯 核心切入点（打中了哪个致命盲区）

🎯 Core Entry Point (Fatal Blind Spot Targeted)

现有表格基础模型（UniTabE、CARTE、TabPFN v2）在面对 d_A ≠ d_B 且列名完全异构 的跨域场景时，均依赖某种形式的隐式或显式Schema对齐。但在金融风控的真实业务中，源域（公开数据集）与目标域（私有银行数据）的列名集合完全不相交，且目标域仅有极少量样本（~50条）可用。现有方法的致命盲区在于：缺乏一个在连续潜在语义空间中进行列级"软对齐"的通用机制，导致它们在维度完全不匹配时要么失效，要么需要昂贵的人工特征工程。

Existing tabular foundation models (UniTabE, CARTE, TabPFN v2) all rely on some form of implicit/explicit schema alignment when facing d_A ≠ d_B with completely heterogeneous column names. In real-world financial risk control, source domain (public datasets) and target domain (private bank data) column sets are completely disjoint, with only ~50 target samples available. The fatal blind spot: lack of a universal "soft-alignment" mechanism at the column level in continuous latent semantic space — existing methods either fail completely or require expensive manual feature engineering when dimensions are fully mismatched.

🧠 方法论与技术创新

🧠 Methodology & Technical Innovation

  • 模块1 · 列级语义嵌入器（Column Semantic Embedder, CSE）：将列名与列内数值分布（均值、方差、分位数等统计量）联合编码为列语义向量 h_j ∈ ℝ^d，利用预训练语言模型对列名进行文本嵌入，并通过可学习的数值感知投影层融合统计特征，实现"列名语义 + 数值分布"的双通道编码。
  • 模块2 · 跨域软对齐网络（Cross-Domain Soft Alignment Network, CSAN）：在源域A与目标域B之间引入可微分的最优传输（Wasserstein匹配），在语义隐空间中为每个目标列找到源列的加权软对应关系，形成对齐矩阵 Π_{A→B}。该矩阵无需人工标注对应关系，完全端到端学习。
  • 模块3 · 异构感知表格Transformer（HiTA Encoder）：以对齐矩阵为先验，通过交叉注意力机制将源域表格表示迁移至目标域的列空间，再经标准Transformer实现目标域内样本的上下文建模，最终输出违约概率。
  • 模块4 · 元学习信贷先验蒸馏：在源域上通过MAML（Model-Agnostic Meta-Learning）训练HiTA，使其内参数中蕴含"快速适应新列结构"的元先验，从而在目标域仅50条样本时仍能快速收敛。
Objective: min_{θ} E_{A,B} [ L_CE(f_θ(T_B; Π_{A→B}(T_A)), y_B) + λ · W₂(P_A, P_B) ]
where Π_{A→B} = argmin_Π Σ_{i,j} c(h^A_i, h^B_j) · Π_{ij} (Optimal Transport Alignment)
  • Module 1 · Column Semantic Embedder (CSE): Jointly encodes column names with numerical distribution statistics (mean, variance, quantiles) into column semantic vectors h_j ∈ ℝ^d using pretrained LM text embeddings fused with learnable numerical-aware projection layers — dual-channel encoding of "column name semantics + numerical distribution."
  • Module 2 · Cross-Domain Soft Alignment Network (CSAN): Introduces differentiable optimal transport (Wasserstein matching) between source A and target B, learning a weighted soft-correspondence alignment matrix Π_{A→B} in semantic latent space — fully end-to-end, no manual annotation required.
  • Module 3 · Heterogeneity-aware Tabular Transformer (HiTA Encoder): Uses the alignment matrix as prior, transfers source-domain table representations to target-domain column space via cross-attention, then models in-context via standard Transformer to output default probability.
  • Module 4 · Meta-Learning Credit Prior Distillation: Trains HiTA via MAML on source domains, embedding "fast adaptation to new column structures" meta-priors into parameters for rapid convergence with only 50 target samples.

📈 Tabular-RAG: Dynamic Few-Shot Retrieval-Augmented Credit Decision Intelligence with Chain-of-Thought Interpretability for Cold-Start Lending

目标投稿 · MIS Quarterly (MISQ) / ISR / Decision Support Systems · UTD-24 / FT-50 管理科学方向
Target Venue · MIS Quarterly (MISQ) / ISR / Decision Support Systems · UTD-24 / FT-50 Management Science Track

🎯 核心切入点（打中了哪个致命盲区）

🎯 Core Entry Point (Fatal Blind Spot Targeted)

管理信息系统领域对AI辅助信贷决策的研究长期聚焦于"有数据、有标签"场景下的预测精度提升，而全新信贷产品冷启动期（无历史数据、无标签、极少决策先例）的智能决策支持问题几乎是空白。现有RAG系统均为文本检索设计，没有任何系统性研究探讨如何将结构化纯表格样本作为"知识单元"构建检索池，并激活LLM的CoT推理来驱动可解释信贷决策，更缺乏从降低认知负荷、提升决策质量、增强监管合规性三维角度来评估该框架对风控系统的管理价值。

MIS research on AI-assisted credit decisions has long focused on predictive accuracy improvement in data-rich, labeled settings. The cold-start period of entirely new credit products (no historical data, no labels, minimal decision precedents) is virtually unstudied. Existing RAG systems are all designed for text retrieval — no systematic research has explored using pure tabular samples as "knowledge units" for retrieval pool construction to activate LLM CoT reasoning for explainable credit decisions, let alone evaluating the management value from the three dimensions of cognitive load reduction, decision quality improvement, and regulatory compliance enhancement.

🏗️ 系统框架：Tabular-RAG 四层架构

🏗️ System Framework: Tabular-RAG Four-Layer Architecture

  • 第一层 · 异构表格知识池（HTKB, Heterogeneous Tabular Knowledge Base）：将源域A中的历史信贷样本（含特征向量、决策结果、可选解释注记）编码为结构化"知识单元"，通过列级语义嵌入（借鉴论文一CSE模块）建立跨域统一向量索引库，支持冷启动目标域B的跨列检索。
  • 第二层 · 动态相似性检索引擎（DSRE, Dynamic Similarity Retrieval Engine）：对目标域新申请人 x_q，同时计算(a) 结构语义相似度（列分布匹配）和(b) 信贷风险模式相似度（基于风险因子聚类），动态融合两类相似度检索Top-K邻近案例构建Few-shot上下文池，并随新样本积累实现在线自适应更新。
  • 第三层 · LLM-CoT 推理引擎（推理+决策+解释一体）：将结构化Prompt（申请人特征 + K个相似历史案例 + 任务描述）输入LLM，激发其生成"风险因子分析 → 同类案例参照 → 违约概率估计 → 决策建议 → 监管级解释"五步CoT推理链，确保每一项决策均附有逻辑可追溯的文字解释。
  • 第四层 · 人机协同反馈闭环（HACF, Human-AI Collaborative Feedback Loop）：信贷经理对系统推荐决策进行审阅与标注，新增的人工审核样本实时回流至HTKB，形成"检索→推理→审核→更新"的闭环学习机制，使系统冷启动期的学习曲线加速收敛。
  • Layer 1 · Heterogeneous Tabular Knowledge Base (HTKB): Encodes historical credit samples from source domain A (feature vectors + decision outcomes + optional explanation annotations) as structured "knowledge units," building a cross-domain unified vector index via column-level semantic embedding (leveraging Paper I's CSE module) to support cross-column retrieval for cold-start target domain B.
  • Layer 2 · Dynamic Similarity Retrieval Engine (DSRE): For new applicant x_q in target domain, simultaneously computes (a) structural semantic similarity (column distribution matching) and (b) credit risk pattern similarity (risk factor clustering), dynamically fusing both to retrieve Top-K neighbor cases for few-shot context pool construction, with online adaptive updates as new samples accumulate.
  • Layer 3 · LLM-CoT Reasoning Engine (integrated reasoning + decision + explanation): Structured prompt (applicant features + K similar historical cases + task description) activates 5-step CoT chain: "risk factor analysis → analogous case reference → default probability estimation → decision recommendation → regulatory-grade explanation" — ensuring every decision has logically traceable textual justification.
  • Layer 4 · Human-AI Collaborative Feedback Loop (HACF): Credit managers review and annotate system recommendations; newly reviewed samples flow back to HTKB in real-time, forming "retrieve→reason→review→update" closed-loop learning that accelerates convergence of the cold-start learning curve.
